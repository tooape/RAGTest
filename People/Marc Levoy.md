# Marc Levoy
---

VP and Fellow at Adobe, leading computational photography initiatives and Project Indigo (experimental camera app).

Previously led computational photography at Google (HDR+, Portrait Mode, Night Sight for Pixel smartphones) and was VMware Founders Professor of Computer Science (Emeritus) at Stanford University. Elected to National Academy of Engineering for contributions to computer graphics and computational photography.

Re-coined and broadened the term "computational photography" through his 2004 Stanford course. Also launched Google Street View and co-designed the library book scanner.

Met at [[Adobe MAX 2025]] - potential connection to [[Lr Home|Lightroom]] computational photography work.
