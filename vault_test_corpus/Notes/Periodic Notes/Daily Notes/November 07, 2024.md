## ADRS user journey deck 
[Figma deck link](https://www.figma.com/file/x3uu4wsF9dHff7xhgGtz8U/Express-Creative-Environments-%26-Personas?type=design&node-id=24%3A11405&mode=design&t=JizmGylm4Z40KORx-1)

**Types of user**
facilitator - Cpro
Restricted creator -> AX user 

**Modes:** 
- *Restricted* - ENT and branding lockdown
- *Guided* - some admin is creating shit and then everone else is just remixing that in a semi restricted way 
- *Flexible* - colors and logos are in place at an atomic level but the actual creation is mostly un restricted 
- *Unrestricted* - randos 

### Restricted 
An orgs brand is protected buy processes that highly restirct what assets can and cannot be used by non designers. non designers can only make simple edits. 

**Constraints**
- Staff resources 
	- Creators have too many design requests to meet them in a timley manner. Restricted content creators must self serve things to lessen the load 
- Strict guard rails 
	- May feel too locked down
- Tool selection 
	- Apps to use 
	- they will be locked into this 

**Affordances**
- Budget 
	- mktg budgets and resources exist to support higher risk campaigns and permanant content 
- Speed
	- Quickly produce something without going through some giant approval queue
- Ease
	- Reusable assets and templates



**Outputs:** 
- info heavy social posts
- one pages and emails
- pitch decks
- flyers and handouts


**Opportunities:** 
- Dynamic home page 
	- app understanding of what they are allowed to work on or work with 
- Rec'd output types and design tasks 
	- Linkedin posts only, etc

### Guided
Users are guided by others in various ways during creation. This can be in the classroom or in B2B where a Cpro would create the assets and guides and then provide unlocked templates for the non designers to work with. 

**Constraints**
- time consuming reviews in EDU
- Tools must be selected for the lowest common denom to fit least skilled user. 
- content must meet the standards of excellence this organization strives to maintain

**Affordances**
- Enablement 
	- Creative expression is unleashed within soft guardrails 
- Idea sharing and collab
- Accountablity is shared between the facilitators and the creators 

EDU and SMB

**Outputs - SMB**
- Social ads and flyers
- website banners 
- presentations 

**Outputs - ENT**
- social posts
- web banners

**Outputs - EDU**
- Class materials (worksheets, etc)
- Class posters 
- Reports, rubrics 
- student materials like about me
- Yearbooks, flyers, posters 

**Opportunities** 
- Content variation which takes into account the B2B or EDU context of the given user
- Reusable templates which allow for brand protection 
- Simplify interfaces for Students 
- Interoperability between express and pro apps so you can take full advantage of adobe ecosystem 
- Idea generation based on collabrativley determined parameters

### Flexible
While brand colors and logos may exist, users have a lot of creative freedom to design and distribute assets how they would like, with no or little over sign by others. They are creating for a purpose with goals in mind. 

**Constraints**
- Lack of resources 
- Lack of time 
- lack of training or skills 

**Affordances**
- Near total creative freedom 
- Speedy path to export, no reviews etc. 
- Freedom to choose tools 

**Outputs:**
- ads, promos, flyers, memes, testimonials, edutainment 
- how-tos
- Recruiting stuff
- flyers, emails, one pagers 

### Unrestricted randos 
users of all skills and ages create freely in their own evn, for their own enjoyment, exploration, and growth. 

**Constraints**
- Resources 
	- there's not really any 
- Free time to create
- Training and skills 
	- YT 

**Affordances:**
- Enjoyment
	- can do whatever they want or feel like doing 
- Flexibility 
- Choice
