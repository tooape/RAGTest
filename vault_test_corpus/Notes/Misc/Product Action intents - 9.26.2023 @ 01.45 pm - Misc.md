# Product Action intents - [[September 26, 2023|9.26.2023]] @ 01.45 pm - Misc
---
PAI are best though of as a more granular form of product intents, where instead of associating a given user query with a product we're able to associate the query with a specific action or capability of a product. For instance a query for "darken image" might turn into a deep link to the brightness controls of [[Lr Home|Lightroom]].
