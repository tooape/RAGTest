# [How Western scholars overlooked Russian imperialism](https://www.aljazeera.com/opinions/2023/1/24/how-western-scholars-overlooked-russian-imperialism)

[[October 15, 2025]] 


## Summary
Western scholars largely overlooked Russian imperialism, accepting the Soviet anti-colonial narrative & maintaining a Russocentric focus. This oversight hindered understanding of post-Soviet conflicts and Russia's imperial ambitions, as seen in the Ukraine war, and needs correction.
