# [beir-cellar/beir: A Heterogeneous Benchmark for Information Retrieval. Easy to use, evaluate your models across 15+ diverse IR datasets.](https://github.com/beir-cellar/beir)

[[October 26, 2025]] 


## Summary
BEIR (beir-cellar/beir) is a heterogeneous benchmark for zero-shot evaluation of NLP-based retrieval models across 17+ diverse IR datasets. It offers an easy framework for installation, evaluation with various metrics, and supports lexical, dense, sparse, and reranking architectures.
