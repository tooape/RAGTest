# [How To Edit Photos To Look Like Film](https://alikgriffin.com/how-to-edit-like-film/)

[[November 15, 2025]] 


## Summary
Edit digital photos to look like film. Use diffusion filters for soft glow. Adjust curves for soft highlights & distinct shadows. Emulate film color shifts via RGB curves & Calibration tool (e.g., cyan highs, magenta lows). Apply realistic grain (Roughness, Size, Amount).
