# [SDC AI Foundation Program Executive Status](https://wiki.corp.adobe.com/pages/viewpage.action?spaceKey=adobesearch&title=SDC+AI+Foundation+Program+Executive+Status)

[[November 10, 2025]] 

## Summary
The SDC AI Foundation Program is actively progressing with significant updates across its core pillars, aiming to enhance Adobe's AI capabilities:

*   **Intent AI:** SRL V6 model has been deployed to production, and MINT v2 on CKG 4.0 is fully deployed across all three regions with integrated testing, improving contextual search and recommendations.
*   **Content AI:** AdobeOneV2 services have been scaled for Express, Stock, and Lightroom, leading to reduced CAT service costs and improved latency for image requests.
*   **Relevance AI:** Express template browse improvements, leveraging collection names as queries, are moving to General Availability, showing positive impacts on CTR and engagement rates.
*   **Evaluation & UI:** The Discovery Hub has been enhanced with new analytics features and a robust evaluation framework for Express Template Search, streamlining A/B testing and model improvements. 


## Details
- **Overall Status:** The SDC AI Foundation Program is \\"IN PROGRESS\\" as of November 10, 2025, with several ongoing initiatives.
- **Cost Reduction:** CAT service costs have been reduced, with a 159% increase in throughput and reduced image request latency from 110ms to 47ms.
- **Relevance Improvements:** Express template browse improvements (Collection name as query) are going GA on November 12, 2025, showing a +0.5pts CTR and +1pt ER increase.
- **Risk Resolution:** Past issues like the AdobeOne v2 multi-lingual model delay and slow Stock asset processing on CKG 4.0 are now \\"RESOLVED\\" or \\"MITIGATED.\\"
- **LLM-as-a-Service:** A refactored LLM-as-a-service has been delivered to allow SDC to serve use cases on various LLM models, aiming for cost savings.
