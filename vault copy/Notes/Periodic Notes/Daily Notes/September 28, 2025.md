## SF Gardening 
Planter boxes for front steps

USDA Zone 10b, and sunset Zone 17 
- Can grow tender plants year round

It will require careful watering and cooling 

### Soil

| Proportion | Thing        |
| ---------- | ------------ |
| 40         | Potting soil |
| 30         | compost      |
| 20         | perlite      |
| 10         | sand         |

Check for drainage holes

### Plants
**Mediterrenan herbs:** 
- [[Spanish Lavender Plant Care & Growing Guide|Spanish lavender]]
	- durability
- [[Creeping Thyme Plant Care & Growing Guide|Creeping Thyme]]
	- ground cover

**Succulents** 
- [[Growing and Caring for Aeonium Plants|Aeonium varieties]]
- Echeveria elegans 

**California natives**
- [[California Fuchsia]]


### Care
**Water**
Water whenever it's dry 1-2 inches down 
Water at night 
water until there's run off 

![[first-year-watering-schedule2.png]]

**Fertilizer**
Begin feeding in march, and continue throughout season 
slow release organic
every 2 - 3 weeks 

**misc.**
Group plants by water needs
rinse the plants for salt water buildup 

### Box Planning 

3 boxes, one herb, one succulent, one California fuchsia
