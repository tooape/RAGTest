- [Wiki](https://wiki.corp.adobe.com/display/adobesearch/SDC+FY25+Product+Summit)
The Product Summit is a strategic, cross-functional 1-day workshop designed to align Product, Program, Data Science, and Engineering teams around Adobe’s vision for innovation in Search, Discovery, and Content AI. This summit will serve as a collaborative platform to reflect on past achievements, share insights, and define the roadmap for FY25 and beyond.

The event will foster deeper integration across disciplines, encourage knowledge sharing, and accelerate the delivery of intelligent, user-centric experiences powered by AI.

Audience:
- Restricted to SDC org-wide

Goals:
- Strategic Alignment. Align on key product focus areas across customer groups; socialize SDC product strategy for the remaining of FY25 and beyond.
- Innovation & AI Integration. Showcase advancements in generative AI, search relevance, intent and content intelligence. Explore new capabilities and how they can be integrated into DMe & Adobe's ecosystem.
