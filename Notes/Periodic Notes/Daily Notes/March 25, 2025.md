## [[Intent AI Home|CKG]] Roadmap

### [[CKG 4.0]]
Goal: debut a new architecture for the graph, combining the rich data of bablescape with extensive fine tuning from our query logging to create substantial improvement in the quality and accuracy of understanding delivered by [[Intent AI Home|CKG]]. 

**Milestone 1**
Beyond just scaling [[Intent AI Home|CKG]] up for more nodes and edges, with 4.0 we're looking to drive a step change in the quality and experience of using the graph. 

This new major version brings a few key additions: 
- Clarifying our graph structure by retiring ambiguous node types like "intent" in favor of more readable ones like "Topic"
- new node types like Location, and Human Attributes to better serve app teams with clear and actionable understanding. 
- Clean up of noisy and lower quality nodes left over from the rapid scale up of 3.x
- Lexicalization for improved understanding of concepts across languages, ambiguous terms like "bat", and morphology of terms. 
- Connecting concepts with a denser edge network with ontology. 

Likewise this lays the foundation for future advancements in multi-cultural edges, easier translations between languages, and compositionality of concepts in later milestones.
